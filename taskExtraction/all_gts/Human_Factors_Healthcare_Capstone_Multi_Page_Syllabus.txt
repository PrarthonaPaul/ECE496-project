Course Description:
This course delves into the role of human factors in healthcare, focusing on the design and
evaluation of systems that improve patient safety, efficiency, and user experience. Students will
undertake a capstone project that addresses a critical issue in healthcare, develop a project
proposal, conduct research or implement an applied project, and present their findings through a
final report, poster, and elevator pitch at the Innovation Showcase.
Course Objectives:
Complete a project focused on human factors in healthcare.
Develop a project proposal with clear objectives, methods, and expected outcomes.
Produce a detailed final report analyzing the project's impact on healthcare.
Present the project professionally at the Innovation Showcase.
Learning Outcomes:
Understand the importance of human factors in improving healthcare systems.
Develop skills in healthcare system design and evaluation.
Enhance project management, technical writing, and presentation skills.
Work effectively both independently and in teams to solve healthcare challenges.

Academic Integrity: All students must adhere to ASU's academic integrity policy. Any form of
