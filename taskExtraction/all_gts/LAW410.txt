LAW410: Legal Clinic Practicum
School of Law
Course Duration: Fall 2022 - Winter 2023 (Full Year)
Course Description:
LAW410 provides hands-on legal experience through a legal clinic where students offer pro bono services under the supervision of licensed attorneys. Students will engage in case research, document drafting, and client representation, gaining practical skills and insight into legal practice.
Learning Outcomes:
1.
Client Interaction: Develop professional skills in client communication and representation.
2.
Legal Research: Conduct thorough legal research and analysis relevant to cases handled.
3.
Document Drafting: Draft legal documents including briefs, motions, and client correspondence.
4.
Case Management: Manage cases effectively from initial intake through resolution.
5.
Ethical Practice: Understand and apply ethical considerations in legal practice.
