Capstone Project Planning Course Syllabus
Description of Course:
This course focuses on the essential steps in planning and organizing a capstone project. Students will learn project
management techniques, develop timelines, and set achievable goals for their capstone experience.

Course Goals & Objectives:
The goal of this course is to equip students with the skills necessary to successfully plan and execute a capstone
project. Objectives include developing a project proposal, timeline, and management plan.
Expected Learning Outcomes:
- Ability to create a detailed project proposal
- Proficiency in time management and goal setting
- Competence in risk assessment and mitigation strategies
